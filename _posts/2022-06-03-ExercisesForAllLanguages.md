## For / while

The following exercises only require a loop (for or while), print(), and variables.
They don't require anything else, like if statements, functions, arrays, etc.

- Display all numbers from 1 to 100

- Display all numbers from 1 to 100 and then display "Finished"

- Display all numbers from 1 to 100, but skipping 1 out of 2 numbers

- Display all numbers from 100 to 1 (compared to before, it's in the reverse order)

- Display 100, then 1, then 99, then 2, then 98, then 3, etc until you display 1 then 100

- Display all numbers from 1 to 10 on the same line, separated by a tabulation

- Display all numbers from 1 to 10 on the same line, separated by a tabulation, all of that 10 times (on 10 different lines)

- Display an addition table 

- Display a multiplication table
