{{% projectDescription %}} {{% projectDescriptionBox name="Description" %}} The goal of this project was to add multiplayer to a Third Person Shooter.

Players can join the same session via a lobby.
Simple AIs are spawned periodically, chasing the players.
The players can shoot zombies or other players.
Health, shoots, death, respawn, and visual / audio effects are replicated.
Items can be taken by players, and are also replicated. {{% /projectDescriptionBox %}} {{% projectDescriptionBox name="Tools" %}} Programming: - C++ - Unreal Engine
