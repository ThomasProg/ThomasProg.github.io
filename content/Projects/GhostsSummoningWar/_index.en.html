Reclaim your hat!
Ghost's Summoning War is a 2D top down real time strategy game.
You can spawn 2 different types of ghosts: - Melee ghosts - Ranged ghosts
Your goal is to lower the main enemy ghost's hp to 0.
But be careful, you have to survive yourself too!
Ghost's Summoning War is a game I made quickly for the Ludum Dare 55, in the Compo category (1 person, 48 hours).
